Use case

User-facing analytics

Rich, bespoke data apps are more valuable for users because they provide deeper insights. Build the analytics experiences that set your business apart — and keep your customers coming back.

Benefits

Unmatched expressiveness

Drag-and-drop tools only display shallow insights and don’t support all use cases. Instead use best-in-breed technology, including D3, to create interactive data apps that will keep your users informed and engaged.

Faster performance, better UX

Data apps built with Observable take snapshots of data during build so your users no longer need to wait for your data apps to load — even for your most robust data apps.

Hosting built for data apps

Hosting and sharing data apps built with Observable Framework is easier than ever. Gain greater visibility with built-in analytics and use security and access control features to keep your data private.
